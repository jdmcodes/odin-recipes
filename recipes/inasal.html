Inasal Recipe


Ingridients

  1. 2 lbs chicken cut into serving pieces
  2. 2 table spoons ginger minced
  3. 2 tablespoons garlic minced
  4. 3/4 cup lemongrass chopped
  5. 1 cup coconut vinegar
  6. 1/2 cup lemon or calamansi juice
  7. 1 tablespoon salt
  8. 1/4 cup brown sugar
  9. 1 cup lemon soda
  10. 1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper

Below is the basting sauce

  1. 3 tablespoons atsuete oil
  2. 1/2 cup butter
  3. salt to taste
  4. lemon juice

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine chicken and everything except the basting sauce
  2. Marinade for 1 hour
  3. Combine basting sauce ingredients
  4. Grill chicken while basting - better if you use lemongrass to baste it
  5. Enjoy!
