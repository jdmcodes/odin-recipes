Gyudon Recipe


Ingridients

  1. Thinly sliced beef (chuck or ribeye)
  2. Onion
  3. Green onion/scallion
  4. Cooked Japanese short-grain rice
  5. Dashi
  6. Sake
  7. Mirin
  8. Soy sauce
  9. Sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Cut onions and beef
  2. Add seasonings to the cold pan
  3. Layer the onions and meat in the pan
  4. Cover the pan with a lid and turn on the heat
  5. Cook for 5 minutes and ladle over a bowl of hot steamed rice
